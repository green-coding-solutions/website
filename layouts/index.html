What is Green Coding?
The term green coding stands for software development with a focus on reduced carbon intensity, rather than classical performance or memory optimized development styles.
At Green Coding Berlin we create a green software community and ecosystem in Berlin. Our flagship work is an open-source green software certifcation toolchain that lets you quantify the energy consumption of arbitrary software in typical real world usage scenarios.
Business Services
We help companies measure their internal softwares energy usage, green it and certify their software.
Developers and Code
We educate developers through workshops of state of the art green coding practices
Open Data
We supply most of our measurements and benchmarks as open data to the community.
